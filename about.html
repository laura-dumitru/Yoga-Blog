Our story

Yoga with Laura was launched in 2018 as an online Yoga teaching business. Laura is a Birmingham based teacher with 10 years experience. We have qualified teachers from all over the world who are here to guide you on your journey straight from the comfort of your own home. Our mission is to help people lead a more active lifestyle and to teach them how to nourish the mind, body and soul.

We are suitable for anyone who lacks the time to head to a gym or a studio or would just prefer to exercise from home. We have over 1,000 happy members who have joined our Yoga Community since we launched the online business. We love helping as many people as we can by providing quality Yoga videos, online support and a variety of classes for all levels.
